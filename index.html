About Me

Alice Wangui Wachira is a skilled junior Data Analyst and Applied Mathematician who is committed to leveraging quantitative insights to pose and address complex questions. Possessing a meticulous and detail-oriented approach, she is an analytical mathematician with a comprehensive understanding of computing techniques, algorithms, and mathematical frameworks. With exceptional communication skills, Alice is capable of collaborating effectively with both technical professionals and end-users, ensuring that data is translated into meaningful business requirements. Her expertise lies in python, Excel, research methodologies, and big data capture, manipulation, and visualization. Alice holds a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics BSc from the University of Nairobi and a structured Masters Degree in Data Science from the African Institute of Mathematical Science. She is currently persuing a Master's Degree in Applied Mathematics at Bowling Green State University. Her strengths include outstanding software development skills, analytical proficiency, and unparalleled knowledge of data organization and operations
